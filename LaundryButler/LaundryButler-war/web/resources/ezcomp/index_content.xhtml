Laundry Butler

Laundry is a tedious and time-consuming task for busy professionals and students in this busy metropolitan we call Singapore. LaundryButler is here to save your precious time and make your life much more easier.

EASY SCHEDULING

Customers are able to schedule and track the status of their laundry tasks scheduling, managing and tracking their laundry tasks all in one dashboard in LaundryButler's website. You are also encouraged to rate the completed laundry service to help us improve the service quality.

NO PRESENCE NEEDED

Tired of having to be at home to send off and receive the laundry items? Using LaundryButler, your laundry box will be physically secured near their doorsteps. SO you can be freed from the worry or staying at home.

SOCIAL LAUNDRY

You can combine laundry loads with people staying nearby if your laundry is slightly overflowing your own box ;). Socialize with your neighbour using laundry! Plus you can save up more money!
